FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the rental requirements?

You must be at least 21 years old with a valid driver's license and your own active auto insurance. A major credit card is also required at time of booking.

Do you offer delivery to my location?

Yes! We offer flexible pickup and drop-off options across the Tri-State area. Contact us for delivery details and rates.

Is insurance included?

Renters must carry their own active auto insurance policy. Additional coverage options are available — contact us for details. We recommend full coverage for luxury vehicles.

Do you offer weekly or monthly rentals?

Yes! We offer daily, weekly, and monthly rental rates. Weekly and monthly rentals receive discounted pricing — call or email us to get a custom quote.

What are your business hours?

We're available 24/7. Call us at 646-407-0553 or email autoconsult273@gmail.com anytime — day or night, weekends included.

What is your cancellation policy?

Free cancellation up to 24 hours before pickup. Same-day cancellations may incur a one-day fee. Contact us and we'll work with you.

Do you offer roadside assistance?

Yes — every rental includes 24/7 roadside assistance. Just call 646-407-0553 and we'll be there right away.

What is the minimum rental period?

Our minimum rental is 1 day (24 hours). We also offer flexible weekly and monthly rates at a discount for longer commitments.
