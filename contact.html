FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What do I need to rent a car?

A valid driver's license, a major credit card, and proof of insurance. Renters must be at least 21 years old.

Do you offer delivery to my location?

Yes! We offer flexible pickup and drop-off options across the Tri-State area. Contact us for delivery rates.

Is insurance included?

Basic liability is included. Full coverage options are available at checkout. We recommend adding comprehensive for luxury vehicles.

What is the minimum rental period?

Our minimum rental is 1 day (24 hours). Weekly and monthly rates are available for extended rentals at a discount.

What is your cancellation policy?

Free cancellation up to 24 hours before pickup. Same-day cancellations may incur a one-day fee.

Do you offer roadside assistance?

Yes — every rental includes 24/7 roadside assistance. Just call our support line and we'll be there.
